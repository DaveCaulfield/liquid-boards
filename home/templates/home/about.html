A local skateshop vibe for a global skateboard tribe.

Liquid Boards was setup by three independent skate shop owners from Dublin, New York and New Zealand.

Demand for skateboards has boomed over the last few years but a lack of global stock and supplychains has limited their availability.

The three skateshops have pooled their contacts across three continents and have combined their online presence allowing them to deliver to a larger community.
