Local skateshop vibe for a global skateboard tribe

Liquid Boards was setup by three independent skate shop owners from Dublin, New York and New Zealand.

Demand for Skateboards, Longboards and Surf-Skates has boomed over the last few years but a lack of global stock combined with supply chain issues has limited their availability.

The three skateshops have pooled their contacts & resources across three continents and combined their online presence allowing them to source and deliver quality boards & equipment for the growing community.

Whether you're looking for American or European brands or skate spots & events in New Zealand get in touch.
